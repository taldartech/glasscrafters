Painted Stained Glass Course

Master the art of painting on glass with traditional techniques

Course Overview

Our 4-week Painted Stained Glass course is designed for those who want to explore the traditional art of glass painting. Learn from master artists who will guide you through centuries-old techniques used in cathedrals and historical buildings around the world.

What You'll Learn

  • • Understanding glass paints and their properties
  • Traditional tracing and matting techniques
  • Creating depth and dimension with shading
  • Special effects including silver stain and gilding
  • Firing techniques for permanent results
  • Combining painting with leaded glass techniques

Course Highlights

This intensive course includes hands-on practice with professional-grade materials. You'll complete several projects, starting with simple designs and progressing to more complex compositions. By the end, you'll have the skills to create your own painted glass masterpieces.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience with glass painting?

While some experience with glass work is helpful, we welcome dedicated beginners. The course is designed to take you from basic techniques to advanced applications. However, we do recommend some familiarity with handling glass before taking this course.

What materials are included in the course fee?

The course fee includes all necessary materials: glass paints, brushes, tracing colors, silver stain, glass pieces, and access to our kiln for firing. You'll also receive a set of professional brushes to keep.

Can I work on my own designs?

Absolutely! While we provide patterns for learning techniques, we encourage students to develop their own designs. Our instructors will help you adapt your ideas for glass painting techniques.
