Stained Glass Mosaic Course

Create stunning glass mosaics for your home and garden

Course Overview

Our 6-week Stained Glass Mosaic course is perfect for beginners who want to explore the colorful world of glass mosaics. Learn to design, cut, and arrange glass pieces to create beautiful, durable artworks for your home or garden.

What You'll Learn

  • • Understanding different types of glass for mosaics
  • Design principles and pattern creation
  • Glass cutting and shaping techniques
  • Adhesives and grouting methods
  • Finishing and sealing techniques
  • Creating both indoor and outdoor mosaic pieces

Project Examples

You'll complete several projects including a mosaic picture frame, trivet, and a larger wall art piece. All materials are provided, and you'll take home everything you create!

Frequently Asked Questions

What will I take home after the course?

You'll take home all the projects you complete during the course, including a mosaic picture frame, trivet, and a larger wall art piece. You'll also receive a certificate of completion.

Are the materials safe to work with?

Yes, we use non-toxic, lead-free materials. We'll provide safety equipment including gloves and safety glasses, and our instructors will guide you on safe handling of all materials.

Can children attend this course?

This course is designed for ages 16 and up. For younger children, we recommend our special kids' workshops that run during school holidays.
