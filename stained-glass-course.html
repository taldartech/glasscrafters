Genuine Stained Glass Course

Master the traditional techniques of creating beautiful stained glass art

Course Overview

Our 8-week Genuine Stained Glass course is designed for both beginners and those with some experience who want to refine their skills. You'll learn the entire process of creating beautiful stained glass pieces, from design to installation.

What You'll Learn

  • • Understanding different types of glass and their properties
  • Pattern design and glass cutting techniques
  • Copper foiling and lead came methods
  • Soldering techniques for strong, clean joints
  • Finishing and patination
  • Installation and maintenance

Project Examples

Throughout the course, you'll work on several projects, starting with simple designs and progressing to more complex pieces. Your final project will be a piece of your own design.

Course Details

Duration: 8 weeks

Schedule: 2 hours per week

Level: Beginner to Intermediate

Class Size: Max 8 students

Price: ₹15,000 (materials included)

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience?

No prior experience is necessary! Our course is designed for complete beginners, though those with some experience will also find value in refining their techniques.

What materials are included in the course fee?

The course fee includes all necessary materials including glass, copper foil, lead came, solder, and tools. You'll only need to bring your creativity!

Can I make up a class if I miss one?

Yes, we offer one make-up class per student per course. Please inform us in advance if you need to miss a class, and we'll arrange a make-up session.
