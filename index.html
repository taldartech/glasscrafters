About GlassCrafters India

GlassCrafters India is a premier stained glass studio dedicated to preserving and teaching the ancient art of stained glass. With over 15 years of experience, we create beautiful custom pieces and offer comprehensive classes for all skill levels.

Our mission is to bring the beauty of stained glass to homes, businesses, and places of worship across India while fostering a community of glass art enthusiasts.
