Our Stained Glass Classes

Discover the art of stained glass through our expert-led workshops and courses

Genuine Stained Glass

8-Week Course | All Levels

Learn the traditional techniques of creating beautiful stained glass pieces from start to finish. This comprehensive course covers glass cutting, foiling, soldering, and finishing techniques.

  • Perfect for beginners
  • All materials provided
  • Small class sizes
Learn More

Stained Glass Mosaic

6-Week Course | Beginner Friendly

Explore the colorful world of glass mosaics. Learn how to design, cut, and arrange glass pieces to create stunning mosaic artworks for your home or garden.

  • No prior experience needed
  • Take home your creation
  • All tools provided
Learn More

Painted Stained Glass

4-Week Course | Intermediate Level

Master the art of painting on glass to add intricate details and shading to your stained glass projects. Learn various techniques including tracing, matting, and firing.

  • Some experience recommended
  • Professional-grade materials
  • Studio access included
